My Story

My name is Jeannette Garea and I am currently an engineering student. I live in Paris, France, in a student residence with my twin sister. I decided to go vegan at the end of the year of 2018 after my high school graduation and a few months of turning defetely into a vegetarian. I've never really liked the taste of meat or even the idea of eating animal protein food, but before turning vegan, I've never questioned myself about why I was still eating like that. Finally, I realized it was time for me to change, to do something better, for the animals, for the planet... and for me.

Once I decided to go vegan, I needed to learn a lot of things, of course. I learnt by myself all the benefits of this unique lifestyle, and the more I searched, the more I learned about Veganism. It wasn't just about food, it was also about an humanitarian act, a environmentalist involvement and a moral cause. Because I wasn't any protein or vitamin expert, I cheked my diet with a nutrionist and I want to encourage you to do the same, if you're not an expert. You need to learn from others too if you want to increase your knowledge. I promise you, this never ending journey you have ahead will be mervelous.
